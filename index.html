Our Services

Professional dental CAD design for a wide range of cases

  • Crown & Bridge design (Zirconia, PMMA, Metal, E.max)
  • Implant restorations (custom abutments, screw-retained crowns, bars)
  • Veneers, Inlays & Onlays
  • Temporary restorations
  • Full-arch and complex cases
  • STL file optimization for milling and 3D printing

Simple & Efficient Workflow

From STL upload to production-ready delivery

  1. You send your STL files
  2. We design according to your instructions
  3. Review & adjustments if needed
  4. Final delivery ready for production

Compatible with all major CAD software and milling systems.

What we need from you

  • STL files (upper/lower, scan bodies if implant case)
  • Case type (crown/bridge/implant/full-arch)
  • Material preference & margin requirements
  • Notes on occlusion/contact points
  • Any reference photos (optional)
